Revew / Parables of Judgment Overview

The church, by and large, has always been more receptive to judgment-as-settling-scores than to judgment as proceeding out of, and in accordance with, the reconciling grace of resurrection.

PotK -> Feeding of the Five Thousand -> PoG -> Palm Sunday -> PoJ

Colors of the Parables:

Parables of the Kingdom
Green, about growing, seeds and plans. About the mystery of a kingdom already planted, a kingdom at hand, a kingdom in our midst
Parables of Grace
Purple, about passionate selfless love. Love that works by death.
Parables of Judgment
White, light of the world. Contain all the colors of the other parables.

Jesus must not be read as having baited us with grace only to clobber us in the end with law.

Master keys to the parables

Parables of the Kingdom
growth in a mystery
Parables of Grace
death-resurrection
Parables of Judgment
inclusion before exclusion; no one is kicked out who wasn't already in

The difference between heaven and hell is simply that those in heaven accept the endless forgiveness, while those in hell reject it.
